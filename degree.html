How to type Degrees Symbol (°)

Degree symbol is °.
Sometimes, we need to type this and can't because it isn't on our keyboards.
Here's how!

Linux

Ctrl+Shift+U and then type B0, now hit enter!

Mac

Shift + Option + 8

OR you can hold down option and hit 0, but I'm uncertain. Sadly, I think this one is the easiest :<

Windows

Hold ALT, and then type 0167 on your numpad.
If you dont have a numpad, and have an Fn key, hold down Fn and use the normal numbers.


One last thing: If you're on mobile or console, just check your on-screen keyboard; it'll probably be there.
